Why full Leather upholstery replacement vs cheap slip-on Seat Covers

Look and feel:
Cheaper Slip-on Seat Covers are not designed to fit your exact vehicle the way Roadwire Leather Kits are. The quality of the Leather and vinyl is usually lower as well. Unlike Roadwire’s leather kits, which mimic OEM interiors to perfection, Slip-on Seat Covers are placed on top of the seats and will never look as an OEM leather interior.

Most slip-on Seat Covers out there are not tested for industry safety standards. Roadwire Leather Kits are designed and tested for Airbag deployment and meet safety standards. Cheaper readily available Seat Covers are well…just seat covers and do not add value to your vehicle. However, Roadwire Leather kits do in fact upgrade your vehicle upholstery. Leather interior ranks in the top 3 most desirable luxury features that people look when buying a vehicle.
